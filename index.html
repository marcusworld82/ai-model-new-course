Course Overview

What This Course Is

A beginner-friendly system for building AI-powered brand visuals from scratch. You will learn how to create realistic AI models, build scenes that match your brand, place your real products onto those models, and turn images into short videos for social media, ads, and websites.

You will use a workflow built around ChatGPT or Claude, Higgsfield Soul 2.0, and Nana Banana Pro. No code, design experience, or photography knowledge required.

Who This Course Is For

This Course Is Built For

  • Streetwear and commercial brand owners
  • Artists, creators, and entrepreneurs
  • Freelancers building content for clients
  • Beginners who want professional visuals without a photoshoot
